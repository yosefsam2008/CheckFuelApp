# Privacy Policy - Fuel Cost Calculator App

**Last Updated: December 2025**

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to the Fuel Cost Calculator App ("the App," "we," "us," "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we use your data.

This Privacy Policy explains:
- What data we collect
- How we use it
- What rights you have as a user
- How you can delete all your data

## 2. What Data Do We Collect?

### 2.1 Data You Enter Directly:
When you use the App, you can enter:
- **Vehicle License Plate** - to retrieve vehicle data from the government database
- **Vehicle Details** - manufacturer name, model, year, fuel type (gasoline/diesel/electric), fuel consumption
- **Fuel/Electricity Price** - the current price per liter or kWh
- **Trip Distance** - for cost calculation purposes

### 2.2 Data Collected Automatically:
- **Calculation History** - every calculation you perform (distance, cost, consumption)
- **Date and Time** - when you performed each calculation
- **Saved Vehicle Information** - your list of vehicles and all their details

### 2.3 Data We Do NOT Collect:
❌ We do not collect your personal identification (name, phone, email)
❌ We do not send your vehicle data to any external server
❌ We do not use cookies or tracking
❌ We do not use GPS or location data

## 3. How Do We Store Your Data?

**All your data is stored only on your device - not on any server.**

We use AsyncStorage, a local database that comes with React Native. This means:
- Data is stored in device memory only
- You own the data - we cannot access it
- When you uninstall the App, all data is deleted

**Security:**
We rely on your operating system's security mechanisms (iOS/Android):
- Data is encrypted by the OS
- We do not transmit data over the network without encryption

## 4. How Do We Use Your Data?

### Only Purpose of Data:
- **Calculate Trip Costs** - calculate fuel/electricity costs based on consumption
- **Maintain History** - save your calculations for future reference
- **Improve User Experience** - such as saving your vehicle as default

**What We Do NOT Do:**
- We do not sell, share, or transmit your data
- We do not use data for advertising or marketing purposes
- We do not use data for analytics or user tracking

## 5. External APIs - How They Are Used

### 5.1 data.gov.il API (Vehicle Data)
**When We Use It:**
- Only when you manually enter a vehicle license plate
- To retrieve vehicle details from the Israeli government database

**What We Send:**
- License plate number only (no personal identification)

**What They Do With It:**
- All information is in Israel's open government database
- You can read their terms at: https://data.gov.il

**Privacy Note:**
- They don't know who you are
- They only see the license plate number
- Each lookup is separate (no cross-referencing)

### 5.2 Fuel Economy API (Fuel Consumption)
**When We Use It:**
- To retrieve recommended fuel consumption data

**What We Send:**
- Vehicle details only (manufacturer, model, year)
- No personal identification

## 6. Who Do We Share Your Data With?

**Short Answer: No One.**

- We do not share data with third parties
- We do not sell data
- We do not allow other companies to access it
- We do not send data to any servers of ours (we don't have servers)

**Exception:**
If you yourself share calculation results (via "Share Results"), then **you** choose who to share with (WhatsApp, SMS, etc.).

## 7. Your Privacy Rights

### 7.1 Right to Access Your Data
You can:
- View all your vehicles at any time
- View all your calculations at any time
- Copy your data

### 7.2 Right to Delete Your Data
You can remove:
- A single vehicle from your list
- A single calculation from history
- All data when uninstalling the App

**How:**
- Select vehicle → tap "Delete"
- Select calculation in history → tap "Delete"
- Uninstall the App → all data is deleted

### 7.3 Right to Control Your Data
You can:
- Replace a vehicle when it's no longer in use
- Update vehicle details at any time
- Clear history at any time

## 8. How Long Do We Keep Your Data?

**As Long as You Want - We Don't Delete It Ourselves.**

- Data is stored on your device as long as the App is installed
- We do not automatically delete anything
- When you uninstall the App, all data is deleted from the system

## 9. Children and Minors

The App is not intended for children under 13 years old. If you have a child in this age group, please review the App yourself to ensure they understand it.

## 10. Security

We use the best practices of your operating system:
- AsyncStorage is encrypted by iOS/Android
- We do not transmit data without encryption
- We do not store passwords or sensitive information

**Remember:** No security is 100% perfect - if someone gains physical access to your device, they can access the data.

## 11. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. If there are significant changes:
- We will notify you in the App
- You will need to agree again

## 12. Contact Us

If you have questions about your privacy:
- Check the App settings (menu "About")
- Or contact us via Google Play Store
